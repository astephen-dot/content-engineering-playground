This document serves as the strategic source of truth before content creation begins.

The objective is to transform a content request into a structured brief that aligns content with business goals, buyer journey stages, audience needs, messaging frameworks, and product positioning.

The AI should complete every section using available inputs, supporting documentation, and organizational knowledge sources.

# **13\. AI Drafting Instructions**

When generating content:

### **Prioritize**

* Audience relevance  
* Strategic messaging  
* Evidence-backed claims  
* Clear buyer progression

### **Maintain**

* Approved brand voice  
* Theme alignment  
* Persona alignment  
* Buyer-stage alignment

### **Avoid**

* Unsupported claims  
* Generic marketing language  
* Repetition  
* Product-first messaging without context
