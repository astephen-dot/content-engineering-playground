```
Buyer Stage:

Blog Type:
  - Buyer's Guide
```

---

# **Purpose**

A Buyer's Guide helps readers understand how to evaluate potential solutions to a business challenge.

The goal is not to sell a specific product.

The goal is not to compare vendors.

The goal is to help buyers understand:

* What capabilities matter  
* What criteria should be evaluated  
* What questions should be asked  
* What mistakes should be avoided

The reader should leave saying:

"I know how to evaluate this category."

---

# **When To Use**

Use this template when readers are actively researching solutions.

### **Examples**

* Workforce Intelligence Platforms  
* Skills Intelligence Solutions  
* Internal Mobility Platforms  
* Talent Intelligence Solutions  
* Responsible AI Solutions  
* Workforce Planning Platforms

---

# **Audience Mindset**

The audience already understands:

```
The problem exists.

Potential approaches exist.
```

Now they are asking:

```
What should we evaluate?

How do we separate marketing from reality?

Which capabilities matter most?

How do we make the right decision?
```

---

# **Content Objective**

The article should:

✓ Define evaluation criteria

✓ Educate buyers

✓ Explain capabilities

✓ Highlight trade-offs

✓ Reduce evaluation risk

The article should NOT:

✗ Compare specific vendors

✗ Promote product features excessively

✗ Discuss pricing

✗ Push sales conversations

✗ Assume technical expertise
