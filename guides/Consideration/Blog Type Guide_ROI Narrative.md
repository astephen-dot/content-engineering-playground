---

# **Purpose**

An ROI Narrative blog helps readers understand the business value, outcomes, and organizational impact of a solution category or strategic initiative.

Unlike a Buyer's Guide, which focuses on evaluation criteria, an ROI Narrative focuses on:

Why the investment matters.

The goal is not to build a financial model.

The goal is not to promise specific returns.

The goal is to help readers connect a solution to meaningful business outcomes.

The reader should leave saying:

"I understand why this investment may be worth pursuing."

# **Audience Mindset**

The audience has already identified:

```
The challenge

Potential solutions

Evaluation criteria
```

Now they are asking:

```
What value can we expect?

What business outcomes improve?

How do successful organizations justify this investment?

What happens if we do nothing?
```

---

# **Content Objective**

The article should:

✓ Connect initiatives to outcomes

✓ Explain business impact

✓ Build an investment case

✓ Discuss measurable value

✓ Help justify prioritization

The article should NOT:

✗ Guarantee ROI

✗ Present unrealistic claims

✗ Become a financial calculator

✗ Focus only on cost savings

✗ Turn into product marketing

# **ROI Integrity Rules**

## **Avoid Guaranteed Outcomes**

Bad:

```
This solution will increase productivity by 40%.
```

Good:

```
Organizations often measure productivity improvements through...
```

---

## **Focus On Business Outcomes**

Prioritize:

```
Revenue Impact

Productivity

Retention

Agility

Risk Reduction

Employee Experience
```

Over:

```
Features

Screens

Technical Specifications
```

---

## **Balance Time Horizons**

Include:

```
Short-Term Benefits

Medium-Term Benefits

Long-Term Benefits
```

---

# **Draft Quality Checklist**

✓ Cost of inaction included

✓ Outcomes prioritized over features

✓ Metrics discussed

✓ Business case guidance included

✓ Consideration-stage alignment maintained

✓ No unrealistic ROI claims
