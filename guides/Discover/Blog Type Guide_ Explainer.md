```
Content Type: Blog

  - Explainer
```

---

# **Purpose**

An Explainer blog helps readers understand a concept, methodology, technology, framework, or business practice.

The goal is not to persuade.

The goal is not to compare solutions.

The goal is not to provide implementation guidance.

The goal is to create understanding.

The reader should leave saying:

"I understand what this is and why it matters."

---

# **When To Use**

Use this template when the request is asking:

### **What Is...**

Examples:

* What is Workforce Intelligence?  
* What is Skills Intelligence?  
* What is Responsible AI?  
* What is Internal Mobility?  
* What is Talent Intelligence?

---

### **How Does...**

Examples:

* How does Agentic AI work?  
* How does skills-based hiring work?  
* How does workforce planning work?

---

### **Why Does...**

Examples:

* Why does AI governance matter?  
* Why does internal mobility improve retention?

---

# **Audience Mindset**

The audience is asking:

```
I keep hearing about this.

What exactly is it?

How does it work?

Why should I care?
```

---

# **Content Objective**

The article should:

✓ Define the concept

✓ Explain how it works

✓ Explain why it matters

✓ Address common misconceptions

✓ Establish foundational understanding

The article should NOT:

✗ Compare vendors

✗ Push a solution

✗ Deeply discuss implementation

✗ Become an analyst report

✗ Assume prior expertise

---

# **Section 1**

## **What Is \[Concept\]?**

### **Goal**

Provide a clear definition.

---

### **Questions**

```
What is it?

What problem does it address?

Why does it exist?
```

---

### **Rule**

Definition should be understandable by a non-expert.

Avoid jargon-heavy introductions.

---

# **Section 2**

## **Why Is It Important?**

### **Goal**

Explain significance.

---

### **Questions**

```
Why are organizations adopting it?

What challenges does it address?

Why is it becoming relevant now?
```

---

### **Include**

* Market shifts  
* Workforce trends  
* Technology evolution  
* Business pressures

---

# **Section 3**

## **How Does It Work?**

### **Goal**

Explain mechanics.

---

### **Questions**

```
How does it function?

What are the major components?

How do they work together?
```

---

### **Include**

* Process explanation  
* Components  
* Workflow  
* Relationships

---

### **Rule**

Keep explanations conceptual.

Avoid technical deep dives unless necessary.

---

# **Section 4**

## **Common Misconceptions**

### **Goal**

Clarify misunderstandings.

---

### **Questions**

```
What do people often get wrong?

What assumptions create confusion?
```

---

### **Example**

Agentic AI:

```
Misconception:
AI replaces recruiters.

Reality:
AI augments recruiter decision-making.
```

---

# **Section 5**

## **Real-World Applications**

### **Goal**

Connect concept to practice.

---

### **Questions**

```
How are organizations using this?

What use cases exist?

Where does it create value?
```

---

### **Include**

* Business examples  
* Industry observations  
* Common use cases

---

# **Section 6**

## **What Leaders Should Consider**

### **Goal**

Move from understanding to evaluation.

---

### **Questions**

```
What should organizations think about?

What capabilities are required?

What questions should leaders ask?
```

---

### **Rule**

Stay educational.

Do not transition into product evaluation.

---

# **Conclusion**

## **Goal**

Reinforce understanding.
