```
  - Solution Exploration
```

---

# **Purpose**

A Solution Exploration blog helps readers understand the different approaches organizations are using to address a business challenge.

The goal is not to sell a solution.

The goal is not to compare vendors.

The goal is to help readers understand:

* Available approaches  
* Emerging strategies  
* Benefits and trade-offs  
* Considerations for success

The reader should leave saying:

"These are the approaches we should investigate."

---

### **Common Business Challenges**

### **Transformation Initiatives**

---

# **Audience Mindset**

The audience already understands the problem.

Now they are asking:

```
How are organizations solving this?

What approaches exist?

What should we consider?

What are the benefits and trade-offs?
```

---

# **Content Objective**

The article should:

✓ Introduce solution categories

✓ Explain different approaches

✓ Highlight benefits

✓ Highlight considerations

✓ Help readers evaluate options

The article should NOT:

✗ Compare vendors

✗ Position a product as the only answer

✗ Deeply discuss implementation

✗ Become a buyer's guide

✗ Focus on pricing

---

## **Goal**

Establish the challenge and the need for new approaches.

---

## **Structure**

### **Paragraph 1**

---

### **Paragraph 2**

Explain why traditional approaches struggle.

---

### **Paragraph 3**

---

### **Paragraph 4**

Preview the approaches discussed.

# **Eightfold POV Placement**

Use:

```
Challenge

↓

Solution Landscape

↓

Benefits & Considerations

↓

Industry Lessons

↓

Eightfold Perspective

↓

Future Outlook
```

The Eightfold perspective should position itself as one approach within a broader category discussion.

Avoid:

```
Problem

↓

Eightfold

↓

Conclusion
```

---

# **Draft Quality Checklist**

Before publishing:

✓ Problem clearly established

✓ Multiple approaches presented

✓ Benefits explained

✓ Trade-offs discussed

✓ No vendor comparison

✓ No product-heavy promotion

✓ Discovery-stage CTA included

✓ Reader can understand available options

✓ Future direction discussed
