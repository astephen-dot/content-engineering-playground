---

# **Purpose**

An Industry Trend blog helps readers understand a significant shift occurring within their industry, workforce, technology landscape, or business environment.

The goal is not to explain a product.

The goal is not to provide implementation guidance.

The goal is to help readers understand:

* What is changing  
* Why it is changing  
* Why it matters  
* What leaders should pay attention to

The reader should leave saying:

"This shift is real, and we need to understand it."

# **Audience Mindset**

The audience is asking:

```
What is changing?

Why is everyone talking about this?

Why now?

How will this affect my organization?
```

The audience is not asking:

```
Which vendor should I buy?

How do I implement this?

How much does this cost?
```

---

# **Content Objective**

The article should:

✓ Explain a market shift

✓ Establish relevance

✓ Provide context

✓ Help readers interpret implications

✓ Build strategic awareness

The article should NOT:

✗ Focus on product features

✗ Compare vendors

✗ Provide implementation plans

✗ Turn into thought leadership

---

# **Article Structure**

## **H1 Title**

### **Goal**

Clearly communicate the trend.

### **Formula**

```
The Rise Of [Trend]

Why [Trend] Is Reshaping [Industry]

How [Trend] Is Changing [Function]

What Leaders Need To Know About [Trend]
```

### **Examples**

```
The Rise Of Agentic AI In Recruiting

Why Skills-Based Organizations Are Gaining Momentum

How Workforce Intelligence Is Changing Talent Planning

What Leaders Need To Know About AI Governance
```

## **Structure**

### **Paragraph 1**

# **Section 1**

## **What Is Changing?**

### **Goal**

Define the trend.

### **Questions**

```
What is happening?

What is the trend?

Who is driving it?
```

### **Include**

* Market observations  
* Industry developments  
* Research findings

---

# **Section 2**

## **Why Is This Happening Now?**

### **Goal**

Explain drivers.

### **Questions**

```
What forces created this shift?

Why is momentum increasing?

What changed recently?
```

### **Include**

* Technology evolution  
* Workforce changes  
* Economic pressures  
* Regulatory drivers

---

# **Section 3**

## **How Organizations Are Responding**

### **Goal**

Show market adoption.

### **Questions**

```
How are leading organizations reacting?

What new behaviors are emerging?
```

### **Include**

* Strategic responses  
* New operating models  
* Industry examples

---

# **Section 4**

## **What This Means For Leaders**

### **Goal**

Interpret implications.

### **Questions**

```
Why should leaders care?

What opportunities emerge?

What risks emerge?
```

### **Include**

* Business implications  
* Workforce implications  
* Competitive implications

---

# **Section 5**

## **What's Next?**

### **Goal**

Project future direction.

### **Questions**

```
Where is the trend heading?

What should organizations prepare for?
```

### **Include**

* Market outlook  
* Future expectations  
* Strategic preparation

# **Eightfold POV Placement**

The Eightfold perspective should appear after the trend is established.

Use:

```
Trend

↓

Evidence

↓

Market Implications

↓

Eightfold Perspective

↓

Future Outlook
```

Do not frame Eightfold as the trend.

Frame Eightfold as an informed perspective on the trend.

✓ Drivers explained

✓ Market relevance established

✓ Organizational implications included

✓ Future outlook provided

✓ Evidence supports claims

✓ Minimal product discussion

✓ Educational CTA included
