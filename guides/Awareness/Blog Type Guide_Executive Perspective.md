Version: 1.0

### **Classification**

```
Content Type: Blog

Buyer Stage:
  - Awareness

Blog Type:
  - Executive Perspective
```

---

# **Purpose**

An Executive Perspective blog helps business leaders understand emerging challenges, risks, and opportunities that require strategic attention.

The goal is not to explain a product.

The goal is not to provide tactical guidance.

The goal is to help leaders understand:

* What is changing  
* Why it matters strategically  
* What leaders should be thinking about now

The reader should leave saying:

"This deserves executive attention."

---

# **When To Use**

Use this template when the topic impacts:

### **Business Strategy**

Examples:

* Workforce transformation  
* AI adoption  
* Skills-based organizations  
* Talent shortages  
* Workforce agility

---

### **Leadership Priorities**

Examples:

* Talent strategy  
* Risk management  
* Compliance  
* Organizational capability  
* Future workforce planning

---

### **Emerging Business Risks**

Examples:

* AI governance  
* Workforce visibility  
* Internal mobility gaps  
* Hiring bottlenecks  
* Succession challenges

---

# **Audience Mindset**

The audience is asking:

```
What should I be paying attention to?

Why does this matter now?

What happens if we ignore this?

What are leading organizations seeing that others are missing?
```

---

# **Content Objective**

The article should:

✓ Elevate the conversation

✓ Connect trends to business outcomes

✓ Highlight executive implications

✓ Encourage strategic thinking

✓ Build credibility

The article should NOT:

✗ Become tactical

✗ Explain implementation

✗ Compare vendors

✗ Focus on features

✗ Turn into a product page

---

# **CTA Guidance**

Executive Perspective CTAs should remain educational.

### **Recommended**

```
Executive Brief

Research Report

Industry Analysis

Webinar

Strategic Guide
```

---

### **Avoid**

```
Demo Request

Free Trial

Sales Call

Pilot Request
```

✓ Executive audience addressed

✓ Future impact discussed

✓ Reflection encouraged

✓ Evidence included

✓ Minimal product discussion

✓ Educational CTA included
