```
Content Type: Blog

Blog Type:
  - Research-Led
```

---

# **Purpose**

A Research-Led blog uses research, surveys, market data, benchmark reports, analyst findings, or internal studies as the primary storytelling mechanism.

The goal is not to summarize data.

The goal is not to publish a report.

The goal is to help readers understand:

* What the evidence reveals  
* Why the findings matter  
* What leaders should pay attention to

The reader should leave saying:

"Those findings are more significant than I realized."

---

### **Market Data**

---

# **Audience Mindset**

The audience is asking:

```
What is actually happening?

What does the data show?

Should I pay attention to these findings?

What does this mean for my organization?
```

---

# **Content Objective**

The article should:

✓ Present meaningful findings

✓ Interpret evidence

✓ Explain implications

✓ Connect data to business outcomes

✓ Build awareness

The article should NOT:

✗ Dump statistics

✗ Read like a research paper

✗ Focus on methodology

✗ Become product marketing

✗ Turn into an analyst report

---

# **Article Structure**

## **H1 Title**

### **Goal**

Lead with a finding or insight.

---

### **Formula**

```
New Research Reveals [Finding]

What The Data Says About [Topic]

The State Of [Topic]

What [X] Organizations Told Us About [Topic]
```

---

### **Examples**

```
New Research Reveals The Cost Of Hiring Delays

What The Data Says About Workforce Agility

What Talent Leaders Told Us About Internal Mobility
```

# **Evidence Requirements**

Required:

```
Research Report

Survey Results

Benchmark Data

Analyst Findings

Market Research

Internal Research
```

---

### **Verification Rule**

Every statistic must be:

* Attributed  
* Verifiable  
* Current

If verification is unavailable:

```
Flag for review
```

Never estimate.

Never create supporting statistics.

---

Use:

```

↓

Findings

↓

Interpretation

↓

Business Implications

↓

Eightfold Perspective

↓

```

Eightfold should help explain the findings.

Eightfold should not become the finding.

---

# **Draft Quality Checklist**

Before publishing:

✓ Research source identified

✓ Primary finding highlighted

✓ Findings interpreted

✓ Business impact explained

✓ Strategic implications included

✓ Evidence properly attributed

✓ No unsupported statistics

✓ Educational CTA included

✓ Awareness-stage alignment maintained

✓ Reader understands why the findings matter
