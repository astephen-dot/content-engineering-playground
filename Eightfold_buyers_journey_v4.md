**Eightfold.ai**

**Buyer’s Journey by Theme**

*Five themes · Four stages · Customer Lens · Eightfold Lens*

**How to use this document**

**This framework represents the demand generation journey** — the stages through which we engage and nurture prospective buyers from initial Inquiry through to Sales Qualified Lead (SQL) handoff to sales. It does not cover the sales cycle that follows.

Each table covers one theme across four buyer’s journey stages. 

* **The Stage Question** defines what we are answering for the buyer at that point in the journey. 

* **The Customer Lens** captures the prospect’s actual question in their language. 

* **The Eightfold Lens** is our differentiated answer — grounded in the messaging hierarchy, brand voice, and approved language from the Eightfold messaging guide.

**Stage definitions:**

* **Awareness —** The buyer is naming the problem; they are not yet actively looking for solutions. Content at this stage educates them on the challenge and frames the opportunity.  
* **Discovery —** The buyer is actively learning what AI-powered talent intelligence is and how it differs from what they already have. Content explains the “what” and “how.”  
* **Consideration —** The buyer is evaluating vendors and wants evidence, benchmarks, and direct comparisons. Content provides proof and differentiates.  
* **Buy —** The buyer is ready to validate fit for their specific context. Content enables a customized proof-of-concept or pilot.

**Products** are drawn from Eightfold’s layered stack — Platform, Applications, Agents, and TalentForge. TalentForge is a horizontal build layer available across all themes. Each theme lists its relevant products in the intro section above the table.

**Persona signals** are embedded in the messaging rather than as a separate structural layer. Theme 5 (Public Sector) is specific to government and public-sector buyers.
